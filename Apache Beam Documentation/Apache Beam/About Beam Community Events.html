Beam Summit is the flagship conference for Apache Beam. The first Beam Summit was organized in 2017 in San Francisco - and has continued since then, thanks to the support of various community members, committers and PMC members.

Currently, the Beam Summit assets are managed separately by various community members. For events in 2023 and beyond, we should move to a Beam-owned model (Beam Summit site within apache organization/, website hosted in apache-beam-testing project).

Meetups

Various community members organize online and in-person meetups for Apache Beam. The promotion pages are usually owned by them. If you would like to hand the management of these accounts to the Beam Community please reach out to private@beam.apache.org
